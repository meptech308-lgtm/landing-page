About VRV IV+

Daikin VRV IV+, supplied and professionally supported by Meptech, is a high-performance Variable Refrigerant Volume (VRV) HVAC system engineered to deliver superior energy efficiency, precise zoning control, and long-term operational reliability. As an Authorized Daikin Dealer, Meptech ensures correct system selection, engineering compliance, and seamless project execution.

Utilizing Daikin’s advanced Variable Refrigerant Temperature (VRT) technology, VRV IV+ intelligently adjusts refrigerant conditions and inverter compressor operation in real time to match actual building load demand. This results in significantly improved seasonal efficiency, lower operating costs, and enhanced occupant comfort.

Designed for commercial, hospitality, and premium residential projects, VRV IV+ supports long piping runs, modular outdoor unit configurations, and a wide selection of Daikin indoor units. Advanced inverter compressors, high-efficiency heat exchangers, and DC fan motors ensure stable operation, low sound levels, and reliable performance even under high ambient temperatures—making the system ideal for UAE and Middle East climates.

Meptech delivers value beyond equipment supply by combining Daikin’s world-class VRV IV+ technology with professional engineering support, ensuring efficient, reliable, and future-ready HVAC solutions.

Why Specify Daikin VRV IV+

  • Proven VRV Technology: A mature and globally proven platform backed by decades of Daikin VRV system development and thousands of installed projects worldwide.
  • High Seasonal Efficiency at Part Load: VRT control optimizes operation during partial load conditions, significantly reducing energy consumption compared to conventional VRF systems.
  • Design Flexibility for Complex Projects: Long piping allowances, modular outdoor units, and compatibility with a wide range of indoor unit types support architecturally challenging layouts.
  • Low Noise & Superior Occupant Comfort: Advanced inverter compressors and DC fan motors deliver quiet operation, making VRV IV+ ideal for hotels, offices, healthcare, and residential buildings.
  • High Ambient Performance: Engineered for reliable operation under extreme outdoor temperatures, perfectly suited for UAE and Middle East conditions.
  • BMS & Centralized Control Integration: Fully compatible with Daikin centralized controllers and Building Management Systems for zoning, monitoring, diagnostics, and energy management.
  • Lifecycle Value & Serviceability: Factory-tested components, built-in diagnostics, and accessible maintenance design ensure long-term reliability and reduced lifecycle cost.
