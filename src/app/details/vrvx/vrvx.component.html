About VRV X

Daikin VRV X is an advanced cooling-only VRV system designed to deliver superior energy efficiency, intelligent refrigerant control, and reliable cooling performance across a wide range of building applications.

Featuring Variable Refrigerant Temperature (VRT) technology in both indoor and outdoor units, VRV X dynamically adjusts refrigerant conditions to match real-time load demand and climatic variations. This results in reduced operating costs, improved seasonal efficiency, and consistent indoor comfort.
