Daikin VRV (Variable Refrigerant Volume) Systems

Daikin VRV systems are advanced multi-split HVAC solutions designed to provide precise temperature control, high energy efficiency, and flexible system design. As a trusted HVAC dealer in Dubai, MEPTECH supplies and supports genuine Daikin VRV systems engineered for the demanding climate conditions of the UAE.

VRV technology—also commonly referred to as VRF (Variable Refrigerant Flow)— was pioneered and patented by Daikin. VRV systems are widely used in hotels, offices, hospitals, shopping malls, residential towers, and mixed-use developments where zoning flexibility and energy efficiency are critical.
