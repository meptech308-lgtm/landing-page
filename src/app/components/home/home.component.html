About

MEPTECH AIR CONDITION TRADING L.L.C is a competitive trader of HVAC products through which we endow with technical assistance for various innovative aspects of air conditioning and ventilation processes over international standards maintained in quality of products and service.

We are specialized in catering to our customers with various engineering requirements in diverse disciplines of Air-Conditioning, Ventilation, Airflow Control, Air Distribution, Fire/Smoke Control, Acoustics, and services. MEPTECH AIR CONDITION TRADING L.L.C is driven by fully qualified & self- motivated professionals, collectively contributing their experience and expertise, to gear advanced engineering solutions.

Our strong commitment towards quality backed by the latest technology made us collaborate with renowned international brands like Daikin, Systemair, Kleanland, JC Cooling to name a few and undertake responsibility to cater to the requirements of various MEP Projects across the Middle East.
