About Fan Coil Units

FWW chilled-water FCUs are built with high-efficiency centrifugal fans and optimized heat exchange coils (copper tubes with hydrophilic aluminum fins) that deliver efficient heat transfer with lower power consumption and stable performance. Units are designed for compact installation height and low sound levels, which facilitates ceiling-concealed mounting and minimizes noise impact in occupied spaces.

Key Features

  • High-efficiency chilled water cooling for HVAC systems
  • Compact design with flexible mounting options
  • Low-noise operation with optimized airflow distribution
  • Durable construction with easy maintenance access
